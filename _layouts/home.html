Customer Analytics in the Cloud

Tweet

Treasure Data is a highly scalable analytics platform which enables organizations to drive enterprise level insights with access to their raw data. With Treasure Data, you can: understand user behavior, get aggregated insights, and segment your userbase. When combined with Heroku, organizations can now deploy customer engagement applications which integrate into Salesforce.com while also having the ability to do data driven analysis.


To demonstrate this, we've integrated Treasure Data into Nibs, an open source customer rewards and loyalty app from Heroku. You can deploy your own version of our fork on Heroku in seconds using the Heroku button below:


Although you can start experiencing this app as a self-contained Ionic and Node.js application, its full power comes through the seamless integration with your business data in Salesforce made possible by Treasure Data.

To get the full integrated experience, connect Nibs to Salesforce using the Treasure Data console and try out a few queries on your raw data. Don't forget to check out the Nibs project page for more information about the Nibs application itself.
